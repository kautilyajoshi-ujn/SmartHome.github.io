Locks

Compatible with your Control4 system, smart locks offer a virtual key to your home, giving you the ability to lock and unlock your doors from anywhere in the world, and alerting you to who is coming and going.

Baldwin

With the uncompromised style and quality you would expect from Baldwin, who introduced the world to solid brass hardware, sophisticated and smart hardware is brought to your front door.

Download Baldwin Brochure

Kwikset

Kwikset’s Home Connect Technology enables the door lock to wirelessly communicate with other devices in the home, and works seemlessly with your Control4 Smart Home.

Download Kwikset Brochure

Yale

Yale Real Living locks are backed with nearly 200 years of security experience. Available in multiple styles and finishes, these smart locks provide a privacy mode for added security and allow you to control access privileges for users.
