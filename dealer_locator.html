What’s a Certified Showroom?

Control4 Certified Showroom Dealers have met a series of elevated standards and are well-versed in the art of "WOW-ing" homeowners, designers, builders, architects—and anyone else who visits—through interactive storytelling, visually stunning displays, and hands-on demonstrations that bring technology to life in a way to help you envision smart living in your own home.

A Pro is the Way to Go

When designed and installed properly by a trained, knowledgeable technician, an automation system is able to perform some amazing feats. Our system plays nicely with over 12,000 connected devices that your Control4 Dealer can orchestrate to create personalized experiences in tune with how you live. With Control4 you get the best of both worlds—the ability for you, the homeowner, to further personalize your smart home after the installation, and the confidence in knowing that the system has been professionally designed and installed, is supported by a robust network, and can be adapted to your needs far into the future.
